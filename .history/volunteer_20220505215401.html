Volunteer!

Bit by Bit is made possible by volunteers who generously donate their time to the program; they are the lifeblood of the equine therapeutic program.

There are many ways in which a person can assist the program through volunteering — prior experience with horses is not necessary. The minimum age for a volunteer is 15 years old. All volunteers, both new and returning, must participate in volunteer training each year.If you are interesting in volunteering with us, please download and fill out the following form. Once complete, it can be returned through mail, fax, or in person
